【阅读】Your Prefect Match

发表于 2018-10-19 | 分类于 英语阅读

He’s not prefect. You aren’t either, and the two of you will never be prefect.

But if he can make you laugh at least once, causes you to think twice, and if he admits to being human and making mistaskes, hold onto him and give him the most you can.

He isn’t going to quote poetry, he’s not thinking about you every moment, but he will give you a part of him that he knows you could break.

Don’t hurt him, don’t change him, and don’t expect for more then he can give.

Don’t analyze.

Smile when he makes you happy, yell when he makes you mad, and miss him when he’s not there. Love hard when there is love to be had.

Because prefect guys don’t exist, but there’s always one guy that is prefect for you.

cause: n. 原因；事业；目标. vt. 引起；使遭受

quote: v. 引述；报价；举证

peotry: n. 诗歌；诗作；诗意

admit to someone(that):对某人承认做某事

eg:

I admitted to having made a mistake.

Hold onto:紧紧捉住
eg:

Hold onto the handle when you are riding a bicycle.

Hold onto your dream.

analyze: v.分析；细察

Love hard: 用力爱

hard：副词，努力地，激烈地

hardly: 基本不

hardy: 吃苦耐劳

hardy woman: 吃苦耐劳的女性

work hard: 努力工作

rain hard: 雨吓得很大

eg:

She worked hard in order to earn other’s respect.

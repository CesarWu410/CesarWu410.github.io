【阅读】I teach

发表于 2018-10-11

I teach because there is a great fulfillment that comes in working with girls and boys.

fulfillment:

n. 履行；实行；成就感；满足

come in:
在…中获得

eg:

  1. She gains practical kownledge that comes in hard work.
  2. He believes that perfection comes in practice.

I teach because I wanted to be a sculptor, and I can do so, by shaping lives for the future.

sculptor:雕塑家

shape:塑造；使…成型

eg:

That novel shapes my understanding of Asian familes.

I teach because I wanted to be a artist, and I can do so, by painting dreams for children.

I teach because I wanted to be a musician, an I can, in giving inspiration to children.

inspiration:

n. 灵感；鼓舞人心的人（或事物）；妙计；好办法；

I teach because I wanted to be a historian, an I can in having recorded something for the lives of great men to come.

to come:未来出现；即将出现；

eg:

I am preparing and studying for all the big exams to come.

I teach because I wanted to be a poet, and I can in writing impressive passages of mankind.

impressive:

adj. 给人深刻印象的；引人注目的

I teach because of the reward I receive when a child’s frowns turn to smiles, or when he/she says, “Now I understand.”

reward:报答；奖赏；

eg:

The hopeless parents offered great rewards for any information on the kidnapper(绑匪) of their kids.

I teach because of the personal growth I receive each day as I venture out on a quest for knowledge and techniques to help my students understand.

venture out:冒险

quest:探索

eg:

  1. Feel free to venture out if you want to kown the truth. If you fail, you can always start again.
  2. They set out a quest for a type of precious stone.

I teach for it is in this where I can see the worthwhile and true fulfillment of living.

worthwhile 近义词：deserving, worthy

eg:

He thinks that it’s a worthwhile task that is worth his time and effort.

Ladies and gentlemen, I teach, we teach.
